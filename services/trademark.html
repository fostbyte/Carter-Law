Trademark Legal Services

Your brand is one of your most valuable assets. At Carter Law & Associates, we provide comprehensive trademark services to protect your brand identity and intellectual property. Our Trademark Services

  • Trademark Search & Clearance: Before you invest in a new brand, we conduct thorough searches to ensure your desired trademark is available for use and registration.
  • Trademark Registration: We handle the entire trademark application process with the U.S. Patent and Trademark Office (USPTO).
  • Trademark Monitoring: We monitor for unauthorized use of your trademarks and alert you to potential infringements.
  • Trademark Enforcement: If your trademark is infringed upon, we will take swift action to enforce your rights, including sending cease and desist letters and, if necessary, pursuing litigation.
  • Trademark Portfolio Management: We help you manage your entire trademark portfolio, including renewals and international filings.
